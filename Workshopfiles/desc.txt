This mod adds the ability to revive your fallen opponents as a special "undead" troop!

Undead have several pros and cons:

Pros:
[bold]Undead have twice as much health as a regular soldier
Undead do not need to be paid
Undead do not need to be fed
Undead are given after every battle for every enemy you or your troops killed
Undead are also given from your own dead!

Cons:
[bold]Undead do not have shields
[bold]Undead cannot use ranged weapons or thrown weapons
[bold]Undead cannot use horses
Undead still cost gold to upgrade
Undead are slow in battle
